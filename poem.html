Poem

This is an original typography design using lyrical words to create visual oddities. This design is space themed so the design utilizes space and galaxy elements. The words are skewed to look strange and "alien-like" while the space around the words are littered with stars. There's also a mix of serif and sans-serif fonts for visual interest. Lastly, there's a bit of editing on the "r" or "star" to make it actually look like a shooting star.
